Abilities
ability name - type of ability - affected by this characteristic
Attack - A melee attack - physical
Defend - block or dodge - quick, armor
deceive persuade - charisma
perceive stealth - looking / hearing / hiding - intelligence
memory - recalling, learning knowledge about - wisdom
Attack spell - A spell attack - spellpower
heal - Healing - heal, spellpower
ranged attack - a ranged attack - quick
damage - damage of spells or non spells - physical, spellpower
Insight: intelligence
Notice that memory, attack spell, heal and damage for spells is affected by how many active spells you have
